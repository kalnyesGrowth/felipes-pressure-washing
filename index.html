FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What's the difference between pressure washing and soft washing?

Pressure washing uses high-pressure water for hard surfaces like concrete. Soft washing uses low-pressure water with eco-friendly solutions — ideal for siding, roofs, and delicate surfaces.

Will pressure washing damage my siding or paint?

Not when done correctly. Felipe assesses every surface and uses the appropriate method. For vinyl siding, painted wood, and stucco, we always use soft wash to prevent damage.

How often should I get my house washed?

We recommend once a year to prevent mold and algae buildup. Driveways and patios may need cleaning every 1–2 years depending on traffic and shade.

How much does pressure washing cost?

Most driveways start around $150. Full house washes range from $250–$500. Felipe provides free estimates with no obligation — honest pricing guaranteed.

Is Felipe really 15 years old?

Yes! Felipe started his business to save for university. He's fully trained on commercial equipment, licensed, and insured. His age is his superpower — he works harder than anyone to prove himself.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Fredericksburg, Stafford, Spotsylvania, King George, and surrounding areas in the Northern Virginia / Fredericksburg region.
